Help

  • Left click the board to place a pixel
  • Use WASD to navigate palette colors
  • Use F to toggle fullscreen
  • Use G to toggle the grid
  • Right click a color to remove from your palette
  • Arrow keys move the cursor (hold repeats slowly)
  • Enter places a pixel at the cursor
  • Hold Shift + drag to pan
  • Mouse wheel to zoom
  • Press 1 Brush, 2 Eraser, 3 Eyedropper, 4 Hand, 5 Ruler, 6 None
